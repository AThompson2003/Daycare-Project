We're Excited To Meet With You!

Lil Tots Daycare Center Hartford is conveniently located just outside of the metropolitan district. We are located near many of the major insurance companies and other corporations. This makes it possible for families to visit throughout the day and share activities, a meal, or just a quick snuggle. Accessing the center is easy. We are just a couple of blocks away from both major freeways. Our parents enter the center through a secure parking garage and keypad entry. Free parking is provided. Visitors with appointments are provided with a QR code to grant them access to the center. Unauthorized visitors without appointments must speak to security to gain access.

Want to learn more about our schools, including our tuition rates? Call us at (814) 555-1234. You can also fill out the form below, and click “Inquire Now”. We will get back to you within one business day. Currently, enrolled families should contact their center directly.
