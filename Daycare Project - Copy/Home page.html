Welcome To The Lil Tots Daycare Center Family!

Welcome to the Lil Tots Daycare Center! We are dedicated to providing a safe, fun, and nurturing environment for your child to learn and grow.

Our Mission:

At Lil Tots Daycare Center, we strive to provide the highest quality care for your child. Our mission is to foster a love of learning, socialization, and exploration in a safe and welcoming environment.

Our Programs:

We offer a range of programs to meet the needs of children of all ages, from infants to school-aged children. Our programs include:

Infant Care: Our experienced staff provides a caring and nurturing environment for your little one.

Toddler Care: We encourage your child's independence and creativity through play and exploration.

Preschool: Our curriculum is designed to prepare your child for success in kindergarten and beyond.

After-School Care: We provide a safe and stimulating environment for your child to unwind and engage in fun activities after school.

Enrollment:

We are currently accepting new enrollees! Contact us today to schedule a tour and learn more about our programs. Learn More.



Thank you for considering Lil Tots Daycare Center for your child's care and education. We look forward to meeting you and your little one!
